# The Feed

The Feed is the central landing place for reading and discovery in Cognirivus. It displays a live stream of content matching your preferences, with controls to narrow down exactly what you want to read.

---

## Feed Scopes

You can quickly switch between three major scopes at the top of the feed:

1. **For You**: Your personal curated feed. It shows items ingested from the **Sources** you follow.
2. **Public**: Globally shared items. This is a discovery space showing public text, link, and media posts from all users.
3. **Community**: Aggregated feed showing posts published within communities you are a member of.

---

## Sorting Content

Rank articles based on what matters to you right now:

- **New**: Chronological feed starting with the most recently published or ingested items.
- **Top**: Ranked by community score (likes minus dislikes).
- **Discussed**: Sorted by comment activity and thread size.

For **Top** and **Discussed**, you can filter by time range:

- **24h**: Past 24 hours
- **7d**: Past 7 days
- **30d**: Past 30 days
- **All**: All-time ranking

---

## Layout Formats

You can toggle between two layout styles using the visual toggle button in the toolbar:

### Bento Grid Layout

A high-density dashboard that packages items in cards of varying widths. This is ideal for quickly scanning headlines, sources, and scores across multiple items at once.

### List Layout

A classic single-column layout, similar to traditional readers or Hacker News. It provides a structured, top-to-bottom reading experience.

---

## Filtering and Search

The filter bar allows you to filter the current feed by:

- **Search**: Free-text search matching titles and snippets.
- **Source Type**: Limit to websites, RSS feeds, or YouTube videos.
- **Tags**: Select one or more tags to view related articles.
- **Source Domain**: Focus on content from a specific domain.

# Collections

Collections are user-curated reading lists. They allow you to group related articles, posts, and external resources under a specific topic, project, or interest area.

---

## Creating a Collection

To create a new collection:

1. Navigate to **Collections** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Create Collection** (or **New Collection**).
3. Provide a title, an optional description, and select the visibility:
   - **Public**: Visible to anyone on the network. Other users can follow your collection to receive updates in their feeds.
   - **Private**: Visible only to you.
4. Optionally, add tags to categorize the collection.

---

## Adding Items to a Collection

You can add items to your collection from almost anywhere in the application:

- **From the Feed**: Click the **Save** or **Collect** icon on any post or source item card.
- **From a Post Page**: Click **Add to Collection** in the toolbar.
- **From an External Link**: You can paste any external URL directly into the collection editor.

When adding an item, you can specify its position to arrange the list in a specific reading order, and write a custom **Curation Note** explaining why you added it.

---

## Following Collections

If you find a collection curated by another user that you enjoy, you can click **Follow**. Items added to that collection will automatically appear in your feed.

---

## Community Collections

Communities can also host collective reading lists. A community collection allows admins (or members, depending on permissions) to submit links, building a shared library of reference material.

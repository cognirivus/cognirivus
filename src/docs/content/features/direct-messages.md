# Direct Messages

Direct Messages (DMs) provide private, real-time communication between you and other users on the Cognirivus network.

---

## Starting a Chat

To start a private conversation with another user:

1. Navigate to their **Profile** page by clicking their username anywhere in the app.
2. Click the **Message** button on their profile card.
3. This will open a direct message panel and create a conversation record.

Alternatively, you can go to **Chat** in the left sidebar and select a user from your active chats list.

---

## Real-Time Features

Direct messages are fully real-time, built on Convex's subscription architecture:

- **Online Presence**: Active users show a green indicator next to their avatar, indicating they are currently online.
- **Typing Indicators**: See when the other person is typing a response.
- **Read Status**: Keep track of what messages have been seen with cursors indicating the other user's read position.

---

## Message Interactivity

- **Replies**: Hover over a message and click the reply arrow to quote and reply to it, creating inline threads.
- **Reactions**: Add emoji reactions (like 👍, ❤️, 😮, 😂) to react quickly without typing a message.
- **Message Editing/Deletion**: You can edit or delete your own messages within 24 hours. Deleted messages display a "This message was deleted" placeholder.

# Support & Feedback

Need help with Cognirivus or want to submit feedback? Here are the best ways to get in touch.

---

## Technical Issues & Bugs

If you find a bug, encounter an error, or have a feature request:

- **GitHub Issues**: Open a ticket in our repository tracking the bug or detailing your suggestion.
- **Console Logs**: If you report an error, please include any error messages from your browser console or backend logs to help us debug the issue faster.

---

## Community Support

Join our official **Cognirivus Community** on the platform itself:

1. Navigate to **Communities** in the sidebar.
2. Search for **Cognirivus Meta**.
3. Join the community to chat directly with the developers and other users, share tips, and see what features are currently in development.

---

## Direct Feedback

For partnerships, security disclosures, or other direct inquiries, you can email us at support@cognirivus.com.

# Quick Start

Welcome to Cognirivus! This guide will walk you through the essential steps to set up your account, customize your feed, and start curating knowledge.

---

## Step 1: Create an Account

1. Visit the home page and click **Sign Up**.
2. Sign up using your email or preferred sign-in provider.
3. Choose a unique username and fill out your profile details.

---

## Step 2: Follow Your First Source

Cognirivus is source-first. To start filling your feed:

1. Navigate to the **Sources** page from the sidebar.
2. Click **Follow Source** at the top right.
3. Paste the URL of a website, RSS feed, or YouTube channel you want to follow.
4. The system will start ingesting recent items from this source immediately.

---

## Step 3: Browse Your Feed

Now, head over to your **Feed** (the home icon in the sidebar). You can switch between three primary views:

- **For You**: Content from sources you follow.
- **Public**: Globally trending or discussed items across the network.
- **Community**: Content posted within communities you belong to.

> [!TIP]
> Try toggling between the **Bento** layout (compact, high-density dashboard) and the **List** layout (classic single-column feed) using the grid icon in the toolbar.

---

## Step 4: Share and Discuss

When you find a source item you want to highlight or discuss:

1. Click the **Share** button on the item.
2. You can add a title, description, tags, or choose a community to post it to.
3. Once posted, other users can upvote/downvote and write threaded comments.

---

## Step 5: Join a Community

Knowledge is social. To find others:

1. Click **Communities** in the sidebar.
2. Browse the public directory and join a community that matches your interests (e.g., _Svelte Developers_, _Tech & AI_, _Philosophy_).
3. Jump into the community's **Chat** room for real-time discussion, or browse their curated collections.
